🍗Biryani Recipe

🥣Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Mint leaves

📝Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Prepare chicken masala.
  3. Layer rice and masala.
  4. Cook on low flame.
  5. Serve hot.
