🍰Cake Recipe

🥣Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs

📝Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Pour into baking tray.
  3. Bake for 35 minutes.
  4. Cool completely.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
