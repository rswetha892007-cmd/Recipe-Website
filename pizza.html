🍕Pizza Recipe

🥣Ingredients

  • Pizza Base
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Capsicum

📝Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce on pizza base.
  2. Add vegetables.
  3. Add cheese.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
